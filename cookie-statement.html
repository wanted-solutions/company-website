At Wanted Solutions, your privacy is important to us. We aim to be clear about our use of cookies on our website. Below, you'll find detailed information on what cookies are, how we apply them, and how you can control your cookie settings.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit most websites. They are essential for websites to function optimally, enhance user experience, and gather site usage data for site owners.

Our Use of Cookies

We deploy cookies on our site for several specific purposes:

  • Essential Website Cookies: These cookies are crucial for the basic functionalities of our site. They enable core functions like security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as they are necessary for the operation of the website.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us measure and enhance site performance. They track page visits and traffic sources, allowing us to assess and improve how well our site performs. They aid in identifying popular and unpopular pages and understanding visitor navigation on the site.
  • Analytics Cookies: These are used to understand how visitors interact with our website, providing information on metrics such as number of visitors, bounce rate, and traffic source. This helps us improve user experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies are used by advertising companies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests. They also limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of the advertising campaign.

Third-Party and Targeted Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services that appear on our pages. For example, we might use external analytics services that employ their own cookies to provide us with insights regarding the site's use and performance.

Managing Cookies

You can manage your preferences for cookies by altering your browser settings. You can opt to disable cookies or delete existing ones. Note that some site features may not operate correctly if you disable cookies. For detailed instructions on handling cookies, visit your browser's help page or www.aboutcookies.org.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We recommend checking back periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
